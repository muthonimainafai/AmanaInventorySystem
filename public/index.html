Feed Inventory

Same day: a second save for the same brand, feed, and bag size adds to that day’s row. New day: one new row is created; quantity = yesterday’s remaining stock + today’s intake, and accumulated bags = previous total + today’s intake (example: 10 left + 20 new → quantity 30, accumulated 30). Use Clear before Save Record if the button still says “Update Record”.

Retail Feed Inventory

Set price per kg and profit margin per kg for each product. Staff on Sales Per Kg must use these prices when a line exists here; otherwise they use Feed Inventory (selling price ÷ bag size). Accumulated profit on each line increases by margin × kg sold on each sale. Wholesale stock stays under Feed Inventory. For Maize (Germ Wishwa), Broken (Wheat), and Wheat (Bran / Pollard), you can set Weight (kg) per opened bag — staff see it on Sales Per Kg and it drives the Total Kgs column when set.

Chicken Sales Inventory

Record chick sales by breed. Minimum order: 50 chicks. Owner: set buying, selling, and profit margin per chick (revenue uses selling price). Staff: Price per chick follows the selling price last saved by the owner for that breed (from their sales).

Customer Details

New record: choose breed, chicks (≥ 50), buying price, selling price, and margin (selling − buying). New sale: choose breed and chicks; price per chick is locked to the owner’s selling price for that breed. Edit: use Edit on a row below.

Record sale (by kilograms)

Total Kgs is the kg left from opened bags for that product that day (starts at the owner’s weight per bag when set under Retail Feed Inventory, otherwise the catalog bag size, e.g. 50 kg, and goes down as you record sales). Each time you save, Kg sold (this sale) is added onto the same row for that product/day (no duplicate rows). Bags sold counts full bags represented by total kg sold (same product/day).
